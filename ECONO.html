Our company’s main goal is to help families that are residing in low income areas and are in immediate need of financial help. The money we recollect for them will come from the government and from private companies. Families will be interviewed and asked to give proof of their current living situation and have individual background checks in order to avoid fraud and to avoid people taking away from others who are actually in need of financial help. Even though there are other types of organizations created by the government to help combat financial issues in low income families, we won’t lower the amount of money that is given to you. There will be a limit on the money each family would receive depending on their situation but they can always count on that money until they can get back up on their feet. This isn’t like a loan and does not require that we get paid back immediately but we do require that the families we have helped get better, can come back and work to give back to other families in need by working and paying your taxes and by sending your children to school to get a better education and them as well can give back to society.
